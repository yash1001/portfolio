More about Me.

I am an inquisitive person who is enthusiastic and motivated to learn new things constantly with a good sense of teamwork and coherence. I see myself as a self-starter who is passionate about products that are innovative, robust, and easy to use, which can be reflected through my projects and work experience. My long-term goal is to create an impact in society by enhancing the usability of consumer electronics, precision machines, modes of transport, and medical products, or in fact, any industry. My graduate studies has helped me to take the first step towards this goal, so my next step is to expose myself to various industry challenges in these fields and hone my skills through hands-on experience.
